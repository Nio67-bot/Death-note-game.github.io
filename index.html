The Beginning

A God Falls from the Sky

01

The Discovery

Light Yagami, a brilliant student, stumbles upon a mysterious black notebook labelled "Death Note". The rules are simple and absolute: write a person's name while picturing their face, and they will die.

02

The Pact

Ryuk, a shinigami monster who dropped the note, materialises beside Light — visible only to him. Fond of apples and chaos, Ryuk watches with amusement as Light decides to use the note to purge criminals from the world.

03

The Hunt

The police close in. Light must hide from investigators, dodge surveillance, and keep writing names from the daily news — all while outwitting the world's greatest detective. One slip means death. Or worse: capture.

How to Play

The Rules of the Note

Read the News

Scroll through real-looking newspaper headlines every level. Find criminals, memorise faces, and decide who deserves judgment.

Write the Name

Open your Death Note, write the target's name within 40 seconds. Hesitate too long and the page smudges — the chance is lost forever.

Evade Police

Patrol routes adapt each level. Use shadows, rooftops, and alley shortcuts. Get spotted twice — you're arrested. Three suspects on screen means danger.

Feed Ryuk

Ryuk's apple meter drains constantly. Collect apple pickups scattered across each level or he'll betray your location to the police — at the worst moment.

The Note Activates

40 seconds after writing a valid name, the target collapses. Watch the news ticker confirm the death — and earn points toward your "God of the New World" rank.

Rise in Rank

Survive 10 days to complete a level. Ascend from "Petty Criminal" all the way to "Kira — God of the New World" across 6 escalating difficulty tiers.
